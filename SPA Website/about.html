About Our School

Nicolas L. Galvez Memorial National High School is committed to excellence in arts education.

At Nicolas L. Galvez Memorial Integrated National High School (NLGMINHS) in Bay, Laguna, we are dedicated to providing a transformative learning experience that empowers students to reach their full potential. With a diverse range of programs designed to cater to different passions and career aspirations, we aim to foster academic excellence, creativity, and holistic development.

Our Special Program in the Arts (SPA) is designed to develop the artistic potentials of students in various fields including music, dance, visual arts, theatre, media arts, and creative writing.

With state-of-the-art facilities and expert instructors, we provide a nurturing environment where young artists can thrive and develop their talents to the fullest.

SPA MAJORS

Music

Our music program offers comprehensive training in both vocal and instrumental music. Students can specialize in classical, contemporary, or traditional music forms. The curriculum includes music theory, composition, performance, and music technology.

  • Individual and ensemble training
  • Regular recitals and performances
  • Access to practice rooms and recording studio

Dance

The dance major focuses on developing technical skills and artistic expression through various dance forms including ballet, contemporary, jazz, hip-hop, and traditional folk dances. Students receive training in choreography, dance history, and performance.

  • Daily technique classes
  • Annual dance productions
  • Opportunities to compete nationally

Visual Arts

Our visual arts program provides students with a solid foundation in drawing, painting, sculpture, printmaking, and digital arts. Students explore various media and techniques while developing their personal artistic style.

  • Studio courses in multiple disciplines
  • Annual student art exhibitions
  • Visiting artist workshops

Theatre

The theatre program offers training in acting, directing, stagecraft, and playwriting. Students participate in full-scale productions while learning about theatre history and dramatic literature.

  • Two major productions each year
  • Improvisation and scene study
  • Technical theatre skills

Media Arts

The media arts program covers photography, filmmaking, animation, and graphic design. Students learn both the technical and creative aspects of digital media production.

  • Digital media lab with industry-standard software
  • Film screenings and festivals
  • Portfolio development

Creative Writing

Our creative writing program nurtures young writers in poetry, fiction, creative nonfiction, and playwriting. Students develop their voice through workshops, readings, and publishing opportunities.

  • Small workshop classes
  • Annual literary magazine
  • Visiting writers series
